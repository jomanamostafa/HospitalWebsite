Room Quality at Sheikh Zayed Specialized Hospital
Sheikh Zayed Specialized Hospital in 6th of October City, Giza, Egypt, offers a range of patient rooms designed for comfort, cleanliness, and medical efficiency. The hospital has 262 beds across different room types, including general wards, semi-private, and private rooms. Each room is air-conditioned and equipped with comfortable bedding, basic furniture, and access to natural light.

Rooms are maintained to high hygiene standards with regular cleaning and infection control protocols. Specialized units like recovery and dialysis rooms are fitted with advanced medical equipment. The layout of the hospital supports easy movement for patients and staff, with elevators and wheelchair access throughout.

Overall, the hospital provides a safe and comfortable environment for patient care.
