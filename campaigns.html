Our Campaigns: Together For Better Health

Our Goal: raise awareness for illnesses that are not well known and support patients during it while running campaigns to fund for equipment and research

Medical Equipment Fundraiser: As a small hospital, we work hard to provide excellent care with limited resources. Many required medical machines are expensive or need upgrades. This campaign raises funds for critical devices such as heart monitors and sterilization units.

If interested, head over here

Community Health Awareness Week: Prevention is the best medicine. We educate our community through our annual Community Health Awareness Week. We also visit schools and community centers to teach children and families about hygiene, nutrition, and fitness.

Contact Us!

Blood Donation Drive: Every few seconds, someone in our community needs blood. We organize donation days at our hospital and local community centers, making it easy and safe for everyone to contribute. Even a single donation can help multiple patients.
