About Us

El Sheikh Zayed Central Hospital is a leading public healthcare facility located in the heart of Giza. We are committed to providing high-quality, compassionate medical care to our community. Our hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by a team of dedicated professionals.

Our Services

  • 24/7 Emergency Department
  • Intensive Care Unit (ICU)
  • Outpatient Clinics
  • Diagnostic Imaging (X-ray, MRI, CT)
  • Laboratory Services
  • Pharmacy
  • Maternity and Pediatric Care
