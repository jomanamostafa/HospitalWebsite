Partner With Us

Partnerships help the hospital grow and reach more people. Community impact changes us for the better.

Introduction

Our hospital is built on compassion, care, and community. While we are smaller than many large institutions, our impact grows every day through the generosity and trust of our partners. By joining us, organizations and individuals can play a vital role in improving healthcare access, supporting patient programs, and helping us upgrade our medical facilities. Every partnership helps us reach more people, save more lives, and strengthen our community’s well-being.

Current Partners

In recent years, we’ve been fortunate to collaborate with amazing partners who share our mission of healing and hope.

  1. City Pharmacy Group; supported our medical equipment drive.
  2. Bright Futures Foundation; funded free check-ups for underprivileged families.
  3. Healthy Life Foods; sponsored nutrition awareness workshops.

Their generosity inspires us to continue improving every aspect of patient care. We are proud to work side by side with partners who believe in making healthcare accessible and compassionate for everyone.

Become a Partner

We’re always looking to grow our network of supporters and collaborators. Whether you’re a business wanting to give back to the community or an individual hoping to make an impact, your partnership can make a real difference. Together, we can invest in better facilities, modern medical technology, and community outreach programs that bring lasting change.
