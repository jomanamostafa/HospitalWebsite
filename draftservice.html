Our Hospital Services

Providing quality healthcare with care and compassion

Overview

1.6; Our hospital provides a wide range of medical services to support patient health and well-being. We offer emergency care, outpatient clinics, inpatient hospital stays, surgical procedures, pediatric care for children, maternity support for mothers, and mental health services. Each department is staffed with trained professionals who are dedicated to providing safe, effective, and compassionate care for every patient.

Outpatient Clinics

The Outpatient Department provides medical consultations, diagnostic tests, and treatments without requiring an overnight stay. Our team ensures efficient scheduling and personalized care, helping patients receive the support they need and return home the same day.

Emergency

Our Emergency Department operates 24/7 to provide immediate medical attention for critical and life-threatening conditions. Staffed by trained emergency physicians and nurses, we ensure quick response, accurate diagnosis, and urgent treatment when every second matters.

Inpatient

Our Inpatient Services offer comfortable hospital accommodation and continuous medical care for patients who require monitoring or extended treatment. With skilled nurses, physicians, and support staff, we ensure a safe and supportive healing environment.

Pediatrics

The Pediatrics Department specializes in the care of infants, children, and adolescents. Our pediatric specialists provide gentle, attentive medical support designed to promote healthy growth and development at every stage of childhood.

Surgery

The Surgery Department is equipped with modern operating rooms and advanced medical technology to perform a variety of surgical procedures. Our surgical team prioritizes patient safety, precision, and compassionate care from pre-operation preparation to post-operation recovery.

Maternity

Our Maternity Unit offers comprehensive care for expecting mothers, from prenatal monitoring to safe delivery and postpartum support. We focus on providing a comfortable, caring environment for both mother and baby, ensuring a positive birth experience.

Mental Health

The Mental Health Department provides confidential counseling, assessment, and treatment for emotional and psychological well-being. Our professionals offer respectful, supportive care to help individuals manage stress, anxiety, depression, and other mental health needs.
