Patient Rights

Right to Access Care
Every patient has the right to receive appropriate health care without discrimination on the basis of race, religion, gender, nationality, age, disability, marital status or source of payment.
Right to Respect & Dignity
You have the right to be treated with respect, dignity and appreciation for your private life, independence and personal choices.
Right to Information & Informed Consent
  • You have the right to clear information about your condition, proposed treatment, risks and alternatives.
  • You must give informed consent before non-emergency treatments.
Right to Privacy & Confidentiality
Your personal health information and records shall be kept confidential and only disclosed with your permission or as required by law.
Right to Safe Environment
You have the right to receive care in a safe medical environment, free from abuse, harassment or neglect, and with appropriate protective measures.
Right to Participate in Treatment Planning
You have the right to be involved in decisions about your treatment plan, to ask questions and to refuse treatment after being informed of the consequences.
Right to Redress & Complaint
If you believe your rights have been violated, you may file a complaint and seek review of your case through the hospital’s procedures and/or legal channels.
Sheikh Zayed Central Hospital is committed to protecting your rights and providing respectful, safe and dignified care.
