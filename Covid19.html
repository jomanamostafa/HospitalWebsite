COVID-19 Guidelines and Safety Measures
Purpose of These Guidelines These guidelines help ensure that every patient, visitor, and staff member stays safe and comfortable during their time at Sheikh Zayed Central Hospital.
Face Masks
  • Masks are recommended in all patient-care areas and clinics.
  • If you forget your mask, one will be provided at the entrance.
  • You may remove your mask in open waiting areas if it is not crowded.
Hand Hygiene
  • Hand sanitizers are available at all entrances and hallways.
  • Please sanitize your hands when entering or leaving a room.
  • All hospital areas are cleaned and disinfected several times a day.
Entry and Screening
  • Visitors who feel unwell (fever, cough, or sore throat) should inform reception.
  • Temperature checks are no longer required but may be done if needed.
  • Staff are available to assist and guide you to the right department if you have symptoms.
COVID-19 Testing
  • Testing is available at the Outpatient Clinic from Sunday to Thursday.
  • Results are usually ready within 24 to 36 hours.
  • Testing is optional unless required for travel, surgery, or special medical needs.
Vaccination
  • Vaccines and booster shots are available at the hospital’s main clinic.
  • Appointments can be made in person or through the hospital hotline.
  • Vaccination is encouraged to help protect yourself and others.
Hospital Staff Practices
  • All staff follow hygiene and infection prevention standards.
  • Medical teams wear protective equipment when necessary.
  • Our goal is to provide care in a clean, calm, and safe environment.
Final Note Thank you for following these guidelines. By working together, we can keep Sheikh Zayed Central Hospital safe, caring, and welcoming for everyone.
These guidelines follow Ministry of Health and WHO recommendations.
