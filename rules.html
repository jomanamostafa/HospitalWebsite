General Terms and Rules
Purpose of These Rules These hospital terms and rules are designed to create a safe, respectful, and comfortable environment for all patients, visitors, and staff members at Sheikh Zayed Central Hospital.
Patient Rights
  • All patients have the right to be treated with respect, dignity, and confidentiality.
  • You have the right to clear information about your diagnosis, treatment, and recovery.
  • You can request a copy of your medical records at any time.
Patient Responsibilities
  • Provide accurate and complete information about your health and history.
  • Follow the instructions of your doctor and hospital staff.
  • Treat staff, other patients, and visitors with kindness and respect.
Behavior and Conduct
  • All forms of violence, threats, or disrespectful behavior are not tolerated.
  • Smoking and vaping are strictly prohibited inside the hospital.
  • Use of mobile phones is allowed, but please keep them on silent mode in patient areas.
Property and Safety
  • The hospital is not responsible for loss of personal belongings; please keep valuables at home.
  • Do not move or use hospital equipment without permission.
  • Fire exits must be kept clear at all times for safety reasons.
Payments and Insurance
  • All patients must provide valid insurance or payment details before receiving treatment.
  • Billing information will be explained clearly before discharge.
  • For questions about payments, please contact the hospital’s billing department.
Photography and Recording
  • Taking photos or videos of patients or staff is not allowed without permission.
  • Photography may be allowed for personal use if it doesn’t disturb others.
Emergency and Safety Procedures
  • Follow staff instructions during any emergency or evacuation.
  • Report any suspicious activity or accidents immediately to hospital security.
  • Our staff are trained to assist in any emergency situation.
Final Note These rules are meant to protect everyone and maintain a peaceful environment. We appreciate your cooperation and understanding during your time at Sheikh Zayed Central Hospital.
Thank you for helping us provide safe and respectful healthcare for our community.
